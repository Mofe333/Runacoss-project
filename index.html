What is RUNACOSS?

RUNACOSS (Redeemers university association of computer science students) is a community of computer science students from different level to get familiar with the department, relate with your coursemates and get information and updates about everything happening in Computer science as a department

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring together all students from the different levels and to improve our social amd spiritual factor.
